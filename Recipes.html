Oreo Cake Bites

Directions:

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Oreos
  • 2 bars of Cream Cheese
  • Blender or food processor
  • Two Bowls
  • Cupcake pan that can hold 24 cupcakes
  • Cupcake liners
  • White chocolate almond bark

Directions

  1. Start by scaping the cream from the middle of the oreos into a separate bowl and putting the cookies into a bowl by itself
  2. After the whole pack of cookies is separated from the cream insert the oreos into blender or food processor
  3. Blend the oreos
    • You do want to blend till the cookies are like a coarse powder resembling the feel of podsoil
  4. Replace cookie blend back into bowl and mix with cream cheese
    • You will need to make sure the cream cheese is room tempature to be able to mix well, You may have to use your hands to mix
  5. After the cream cheese and blended oreos are mixed, Chill oreo mixture for 15 minutes
  6. Take your almond bark and place into bowl with the cream from the cookies from earlier
  7. Put almond bark and cream filling mix into the mircowave until melted through and through
  8. While chocolate melts, take your chilled oreo mixture and roll into palm sized balls
  9. After all the mixture is rolled, Place into cupcake liners in cupcake tray
  10. Take a spoon and dip of drizzle white chocolate onto your oreo balls
  11. After all oreo balls are completely covered with white chocolate, if you have leftover oreo crumbs, sprinkle some on the top of the oreo balls
  12. Place finished oreo balls into a freazer from 1 hour
  13. After 1 hour, You are ready to enjoy a nice cool oreo ball!
